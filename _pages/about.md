I am a **Postdoctoral Research Fellow** at the [Australian Institute for Machine Learning](https://www.adelaide.edu.au/aiml/) at the University of Adelaide, working with **Prof. Javen Shi**. My research lies at the intersection of **machine learning** and **computational chemistry**, with a focus on applying these methods to **renewable-energy materials discovery** and **drug discovery**.

Before this position, I completed my **PhD in Computer Science** at the [University of Newcastle, Australia](https://www.newcastle.edu.au) (Supervisors: *Prof. Alister Page, Prof. Raymond Chiong*). Prior to that, I earned my **Master’s degree in Renewable Energy** from [Huazhong University of Science and Technology](https://english.hust.edu.cn) (Supervisors: *Prof. Jian Li, Prof. Lichao Jia*). I also gained industry experience as an **algorithm intern** at [Galixir Technology](https://www.galixir.com/en/about).

In my spare time, I enjoy spending time with my sons and exploring nature.
